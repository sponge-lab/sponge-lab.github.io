Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to be admitted to HKUST before contacting the lab?

A: For PhD/MPhil positions, we encourage you to contact us before applying to HKUST. We can provide guidance on the application process and support strong candidates.

Q: What programming languages should I know?

A: Python is essential for most positions. C/C++ and CUDA are highly valuable for systems and hardware research. Familiarity with ML frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow is expected.

Q: Can international students apply?

A: Absolutely! We welcome applications from students worldwide. HKUST has a diverse international community and provides excellent support for international students.

Q: Are there opportunities for industry collaboration?

A: Yes! We maintain strong partnerships with leading tech companies and encourage internships and collaborative projects that provide real-world impact.
