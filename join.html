Open Positions

PhD Students

Available positions: 2-3 positions for Spring/Fall 2026.

Requirements:

  • Strong background in at least one of the following areas: (1) computer architecture and systems, (2) parallel programming, or (3) deep learning (with a particular focus on large language models and neural rendering).
  • Excellent programming skills (Python, C/C++, CUDA).
  • Good communication and writing skills in English.

Preferred:

  • Research experience in AI/ML algorithms, hardware architecture, or systems.
  • Publications in relevant conferences or journals.

Interns/Research Assistants

Available positions: Several positions throughout the year.

Requirements:

  • Strong academic performance.
  • Programming experience (Python required; CUDA/C/C++ preferred).
  • Regular commitment of at least one semester.
