Research Focus

Our research focuses on developing efficient, adaptable, and scalable AI systems through innovative co-design approaches across three key areas:

Efficient AI Algorithms

We develop novel algorithms that optimize computational efficiency while maintaining high performance. Our work includes sparse neural networks, quantization techniques, and adaptive learning algorithms.

Adaptive AI Hardware Architectures

We design hardware architectures that can dynamically adapt to different workloads and applications, including reconfigurable computing platforms and domain-specific accelerators.

Scalable AI Infrastructure and Systems

We build scalable systems and infrastructure that enable efficient deployment and management of AI applications across different scales, from edge devices to large-scale data centers.
