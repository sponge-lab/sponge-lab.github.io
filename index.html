Welcome to the Sponge Computing Lab at HKUST!

The Sponge Computing Lab in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST focuses on developing efficient, adaptable, and scalable AI systems through co-design across algorithms, hardware, and infrastructure.

We are looking for self-motivated students who are passionate about exploring efficient AI algorithms, adaptable AI hardware architectures, and scalable AI infrastructure and systems. Prospective students are encouraged to visit our Research and Publications pages to learn more about our research focus and recent work, and to follow the instructions on the Join Us page to apply.
