Welcome to the Sponge Computing Lab at HKUST!

The Sponge Computing Lab in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST focuses on developing efficient, adaptable, and scalable AI systems through co-design across algorithms, hardware, and infrastructure.

We are looking for self-motivated students who are passionate about exploring efficient AI algorithms, adaptive AI hardware architectures, and scalable AI infrastructure and systems to join us. Please check our research page and publications to learn more about our research focus and recent work.
